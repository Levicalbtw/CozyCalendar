# Product Requirements: Cozy Calendar

## Product Overview
**App Name:** Cozy Calendar
**Tagline:** Organize your life exactly how you want it, making productivity feel like a creative release instead of a chore.
**Launch Goal:** Successfully finish and launch the project, get some initial signups, and gather positive feedback.
**Target Launch:** 2 weeks

## Primary User Story
"Meet Alex, a creative student who struggles with finding a tool that really adds structure to their life because everything out there is just a boring grid. Every day they feel unmotivated to plan. They need a customizable, scrapbook-like workspace so they can structure their life exactly how they need it and actually enjoy the process."

## Must-Have Features (MVP)
1. **Integrated Calendar & Journal Interface**
   - Users can click on a date and see both a scheduling area and a free-text journaling area.
   - Includes analog page transitions (flipping/ripping).
2. **Extreme Visual Customization (Scrapbook Engine)**
   - Users can change the background color/pattern.
   - Users can freely drag and drop stickers/images onto the page.
3. **Ultimate User-Friendliness**
   - Frictionless UI that requires zero tutorials. Anyone can add an entry/sticker within 10 seconds.

## NOT in MVP
- Undecided Future Features (saving for later). No social sharing, no complicated mobile-native app ports, no complex API integrations outside of Supabase.

## Success Metrics
- Launch Status: 100% Complete (live web URL in 14 days)
- Signups: 10-50 initial users tracking via Supabase.
- Qualitative Feedback: Positive sentiment via DMs or short form.

## Look & Feel
- **Warmth:** Soft colors, cozy aesthetics, avoiding tech-startup blues/grays.
- **Tactile:** Elements feel like physical objects (paper textures, drop shadows, page flips).
- **Unstructured:** Clear grid for schedule, but completely visually breakable by users with their custom stickers and journal aesthetic.
